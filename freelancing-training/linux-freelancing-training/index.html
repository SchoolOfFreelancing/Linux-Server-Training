Guaranteed Income Conditions

Our Guaranteed Minimum Income (GMI) path is performance-backed and requires adherence to two specific conditions:

  • Regular Attendance: Trainees must attend all educational sessions sequentially. The guarantee is voided if the student drops out due to personal issues or technical glitches.
  • Active Marketplace Engagement: Trainees must purchase the necessary bids/connects on freelance platforms to actively apply for server administration contracts.

System & Participation Requirements

Hardware Benchmarks

  • OS: Ubuntu Desktop (24.04 or 26.04 LTS recommended).
  • Processor: 64-bit dual-core (Minimum 4 GHz).
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM minimum (16 GB RAM highly recommended).
  • Storage: 200 GB available disk space.

Connectivity & Legal

  • Reliable fiber-optic internet with a Real IP or direct server access.
  • A registered domain name and a valid government-issued ID.
  • Portable messaging devices for 24/7 client communication.
