What Is Jasmin SMS Gateway Support?

Jasmin SMS Gateway Support is School of Freelancing's done-for-you service for deploying Jasmin — an open-source SMS gateway that sends and receives SMS traffic over SMPP — together with playSMS, the web-based interface built on top of it for managing and sending bulk SMS campaigns. An experienced engineer installs and configures both, sets up the SMPP connector, and tests message routing and delivery, so you get a production-ready bulk SMS system for legitimate business SMS delivery without building it yourself.
