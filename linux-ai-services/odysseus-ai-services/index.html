What Is Odysseus AI Workspace Support?

Odysseus AI Workspace Support is School of Freelancing's done-for-you service for the Odysseus AI Workspace, a self-hosted AI workspace that runs entirely on your own hardware rather than a vendor's cloud. We handle installation, configuration, and troubleshooting remotely, so you get a working, tuned workspace on your own infrastructure without doing the setup yourself.
