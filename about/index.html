Mission & Vision

Why We're Obsessed With Linux

Linux quietly runs most of the world's servers, cloud platforms, and DevOps pipelines. We exist to put that power — and the income it creates — directly into your hands.

🎯

Our Mission

To make professional Linux skills accessible to anyone, anywhere, and to turn those skills into real, dependable income — for individuals building a freelance career and for businesses that rely on Linux infrastructure every day.

We deliver on this through two pillars:

  • Linux Freelancing Training — practical courses in server administration, DevOps, cloud infrastructure, and automation, paired with the freelance business skills (proposals, pricing, client communication) needed to actually get hired on Upwork, Fiverr, and Toptal.
  • Linux Cloud Support — hands-on, expert support for businesses: VPS setup, server hardening, migrations, monitoring, and emergency troubleshooting, delivered by the same specialists who train our students.
🔭

Our Vision

A world where Linux literacy is the fastest, most reliable on-ramp into the global freelance economy, and where no business ever loses time or revenue to a server they don't understand.

Concretely, we're working toward a future where:

  • Any motivated beginner, regardless of location, degree, or background, can become a confident, employable Linux freelancer in months, not years.
  • School of Freelancing is the first name businesses think of when they need affordable, trustworthy Linux and cloud support.
  • Our graduates form a global, self-sustaining community of Linux freelancers who hire, refer, and mentor one another for life.
